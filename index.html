Top Cryptocurrencies

Cryptocurrency is a digital or virtual form of currency that uses cryptography for security. Unlike traditional currencies, cryptocurrencies are decentralized systems based on blockchain technology. Here are the top 5 cryptocurrencies by market capitalization:

  • Bitcoin (BTC)

    The first and most well-known cryptocurrency, created in 2009. Bitcoin remains the largest cryptocurrency by market cap.

    Market Cap: $880.5B
  • Ethereum (ETH)

    A decentralized platform that enables smart contracts and decentralized applications (DApps).

    Market Cap: $290.8B
  • Binance Coin (BNB)

    The native cryptocurrency of Binance Chain and Binance Smart Chain.

    Market Cap: $45.2B
  • Solana (SOL)

    A high-performance blockchain platform known for its fast transaction speeds and low fees.

    Market Cap: $42.1B
  • XRP (XRP)

    A digital payment network and protocol developed by Ripple Labs.

    Market Cap: $35.6B
